Notes & AI

Type it, write it, or let AI shape it.

Rich text and true Apple Pencil handwriting on beautiful paper styles. Turn handwriting into text, then chat with your notes, summarize, translate, continue or improve them — even turn a note into a presentation, quiz or flashcards.

✍️ Apple Pencil💬 Chat with your notes ✨ Summarize · translate · rewrite🔤 Handwriting → text 📊 Note → presentation📄 Templates & paper styles
Tasks, Calendar & Reminders

Plan your day, your way.

A full task manager with projects, areas and smart lists (Today, Upcoming, Inbox) shown as board, list, calendar, timeline and stats. Add sub-tasks, recurring tasks and reminders — including location reminders that nudge you when you arrive at or leave a place. Two-way sync with Apple Calendar.

📋 Projects & smart lists🗂️ Board · List · Calendar · Timeline 📍 Location reminders🔁 Recurring tasks 🔔 Reminders🍎 Apple Calendar sync
Library, Scanner & Import

Your whole library, in one place.

Import and read PDFs, Office files and EPUB books, annotate with Apple Pencil, and pick up right where you left off. Scan documents with your camera, extract text with OCR, and bring your Notability & GoodNotes backups over from Google Drive, iCloud or Files.

📚 PDF · Office · EPUB reader📷 Document scanner 🔎 OCR text recognition📥 Import Notability & GoodNotes🗂️ Folders
Capture & Study

Catch every idea. Remember more.

Record voice notes and meetings with real-time transcription and AI summaries — audio even stays in sync with your handwriting. Study smarter with spaced-repetition flashcards, a daily streak and study stats. Capture anything in a tap with Siri, widgets or the share sheet.

🎙️ Voice & meeting recorder📝 Live transcription + AI summary 🎴 Flashcards (spaced repetition)🔥 Streak & study stats ⚡ Siri · Widgets · Share sheet

Use Apple, Google or email, or continue without an account. Your notes sync across devices with iCloud.
