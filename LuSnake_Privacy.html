LuSnake — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

LuSnake ("the App", "we", "us") is a free, ad-supported retro snake game. This policy explains what data the App processes and why. By using LuSnake you agree to this policy.

1. No account, no personal sign-up

The App does not require an account. We do not ask for your name, email or phone number. Your game progress — best score, daily streak, score history, settings, missions and unlocked themes — is stored only on your device and is removed when you delete the App.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, AdMob may process technical data such as device identifiers (including the advertising identifier / IDFA), approximate (coarse) location, and ad-interaction and diagnostic data. This data is used for third-party advertising and is not linked to a personal account by us.

Learn more about how Google uses data: policies.google.com/privacy.

3. Your tracking choices

On iOS you are asked for explicit permission before any cross-app tracking (App Tracking Transparency). If you decline, you will see non-personalized ads and the game keeps working normally. In the European Economic Area, the UK and similar regions, a consent form (Google User Messaging Platform) lets you set your preferences, and you can change them later from the in-app "Privacy options" entry.

4. What we do not do

We do not sell your data, and we do not collect contacts, photos, microphone or precise GPS location.

5. Children

LuSnake is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

7. Contact

Questions? Email support@lunote.app.
