LuBite — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

LuBite ("the App", "we", "us") is a calorie-tracking app that estimates the calories of your meals from photos. This policy explains what data we process and why. By using LuBite you agree to this policy.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data: your email address. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique identifier and (where you permit it) your name and email.
  • Profile: age, sex, height, weight, activity level and goal — used to calculate your daily calorie target.
  • Nutrition logs: the meals you add, their calorie/macro values, and your water and weight entries.
  • Health & fitness (Apple Health): if you grant permission, we read your steps, active energy and exercise minutes to personalize your daily goal. This data is never used for advertising and is never shared with third parties.
  • Marketing preference: used only if you explicitly opt in to receive campaign emails.

2. Photos — not stored

When you photograph a meal or ingredients, the image is sent through our secure backend to an AI service (Anthropic Claude) for calorie/recipe analysis and is then immediately discarded. Photos are never stored on your device or on our servers — only the resulting nutrition values (e.g. calories, protein) are saved.

3. How we use your data

To provide calorie tracking, calculate your daily goal, produce statistics, deliver the reminders you enabled, process subscriptions, and keep the service secure. We do not sell your data and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

4. Service providers

  • Supabase — authentication and database (data storage).
  • Anthropic (Claude) — photo analysis (images are processed without being stored).
  • Apple & Google — optional Sign in with Apple / Google.
  • Apple — subscription payments (no payment card data is stored by us).
  • Email delivery provider — sending confirmation and password-reset emails.

5. Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account at any time from Profile → Account → Delete Account. Deletion immediately and irreversibly removes your account and associated data; for Sign in with Apple users, your Apple token is also revoked.

6. Local data

The App also stores your logs locally on your device. Removing the App removes this local copy.

7. Children

LuBite is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Your rights

You may access, correct, export or delete your data. To exercise these rights, use in-app account deletion or contact us below.

9. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
