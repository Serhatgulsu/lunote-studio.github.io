Privacy Policy

Gizlilik Politikası

LuNote Studio · Last updated: June 2026Son güncelleme: Haziran 2026

1. Data We Collect

LuNote stores all your notes, tasks, and recordings locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit personal data to our servers without your explicit consent.

2. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud backup, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account and subject to Apple's privacy policy.

3. AI Features

Meeting transcription and AI summaries use on-device processing where possible. Audio sent for processing is not stored or used for training.

4. Analytics

We use anonymous, aggregated analytics (crash reports, feature usage) to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.

5. Third-Party Services

LuNote uses RevenueCat for subscription management and OpenWeatherMap for weather data. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Contact

For privacy questions: support@lunote.app

FAQ

How do I restore purchases?

LuNote → Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases.

How do I cancel my subscription?

iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → LuNote → Cancel.

Is my data backed up?

Yes — LuNote → Settings → Backup & Restore for manual backups.

Does LuNote work on iPhone?

Yes! Designed for both iPad (full Apple Pencil support) and iPhone.
